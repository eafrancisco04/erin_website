About

Artist's Bio

Erin Lee started performing at a very young age, but they only started releasing music in 2020 with their debut EP “Paper Flowers”. Shortly after, singer-songwriter Erin Lee has never let their experimental style of vocal harmony and instrumentation keep them within the confines of a single genre.

Using their creativity and versatility as a self-taught producer to branch out into the OPM scene even further, they are one of the faces that make up two other side projects—namely, acoustic duo CJ & Erin, and Lilystars Records' indie-folk duo Lily On The River—both of which have released their debut singles “Lihim” and “Unhappy Serenity” back-to-back in January 2021.

Right now, working as an audio producer/editor for a local podcast network company and as a freelance producer and mixing engineer, Erin also finds enjoyment in helping other artists achieve the sound they're looking for in their music.
